## üß† **UX Assumptions**

- Users book **same-day hyperlocal deliveries**  
- Service area limited to **Mumbai city**  
- Express delivery available on serviceable routes  
- Package size & weight determine vehicle suitability  
- Pricing updates dynamically based on selections  

---

## ‚ö° **Edge Cases Implemented**

‚úÖ **Express vs Normal Delivery Clarity**  
Price difference shown clearly.

‚úÖ **Unserviceable Address Handling**  
Invalid locations (e.g., Powai Lake, Marine Drive) show guidance & disable Next.

‚úÖ **Dynamic Price Update**  
Fare updates after changing vehicle or package details.

‚úÖ **Edit Previous Steps Retains State**  
Users can go back without losing entered data.

‚úÖ **Form Validation Before Confirmation**  
Sender & Receiver details validated before booking.
